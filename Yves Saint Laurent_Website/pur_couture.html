ROUGE PUR COUTURE

LATEST PROMOTIONAL VIDEO




DESCRIPTION & BENEFITS

KEYWORDS

Rich and highly pigment color. Intense hydration and antioxidant care. High color payoff.

BENEFITS

Achieve pure color in a satin or matte finish. Hydrospheres and natural extracts provide all-day comfort and hydration.

TYPE

Lipstick in satin finishes.

WHAT IT IS

Our wardrobe of iconic lipsticks deliver rich and luxurious color in satin finishes.

WHAT IT DOES

In just one stroke, luxurious and highly pigmented color dresses the lips with intense hydration and antioxidant care. In a signature couture jewel-like packaging, Rouge Pur Couture delivers the promise of edgy style and ultimate feminine strength.


HOW TO APPLY

Smooth the lip contour with Touche Éclat before defining it with Dessin des Levres lip liner. Apply Rouge Pur Couture starting at the center of the lip and gliding to the corners. Then reverse the movement for maximum color impact.
