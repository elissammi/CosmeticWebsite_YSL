THE YSL BEAUTÉ SPIRIT

Youth, audacity, freedom, and subversion—the values upheld by all at YSL since its inception—are the four cornerstones that inspired us, strengthened our foundation and made us the trend-setting, rule-breaking brand we are today. By defying convention, we gave life to a unique style that perfectly balances the want for elegance and the need for freedom. Today, whether it is for him or for her, men and women alike embrace that unique style.

Behold, the collections that define YSL Beauté.

DARE TO BE BOLD

"I wanted a face for the woman I dress," said the late Monsieur Yves Saint Laurent. That very statement revolutionized YSL, giving birth to their beauty arm in 1978, making them the first cosmetics and fragrance business to have a fashion house’s name attached to it. Since then, YSL Beauté has been synonymous with iconic beauty must-haves by makeup artists and beauty lovers around the world.

With singer-songwriter Dua Lipa as the face and voice of YSL Beauté’s new It-conic fragrance of freedom, Libre Eau de Parfum is cool and untamed with an edgy signature style, giving women the power to express their identity.
